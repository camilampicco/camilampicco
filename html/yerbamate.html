Yerba Mate

Yerba Mate has been used for thousands of years by the Guaraní People
for stamina and as a natural, healthy remedy with numerous health benefits.
Today people still swear by yerba mate as a source of energy, as a way of aiding weight loss
and for general well-being. Many people opt for yerba mate to replace coffee as they prefer
the effect of the caffeine found in mate rather than the caffeine found in coffee.

Argenshop is proud to bring you the widest selection of yerba mate, gourds and accessories in the UK.
